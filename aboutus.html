Project Reflection:

This was a fun project. I had worked with HTML lightly in the past but never with CSS and never with embedding JavaScript or analytics. I had to stop myself or I would just keep adding features until I spent 1000 hours on it. I used professor Daniels' example for the analytics piece and threw some ballpark numbers in there from google to get a graphic up. One thing I couldn't get to work right was the setting the max and min on the y axis, not sure why it won't take, would love to hear feedback. For the rest of the project I pulled from different examples I found searching around the internet. I bootstrapped a form for the guestbook then added some functionality with JS to satisfy the CRUD requirement. Some customization too. I made a simple guess who game with some added trivia hidden by css functionality to the home page.

Considerations for improvements for this are nearly endless. First, I would dedicate more time to getting the call to the main.js to function properly such that the scripting can be left in it's own file. Second, I would bring in a light database to support the guest book and analytics. Possibly sqLite ot MySql. Could even be supported by csv files. The gray scale is minimally okay, I would like to explore more color palletes. But this is something where I would spend 90% of my time trying to perfect the last 5% of the project. I would spend too much time on it. Gray scale works. More background graphics and animations throughout the site would liven it up as well.

Again, I really enjoyed this project. This is my first real exposure to front end development and I like it more than I expected.
